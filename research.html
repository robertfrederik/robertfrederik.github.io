A representation-theoretic approach to quantum error correction for qudits
 Prof Dorian Gangloff, University of Cambridge, Summer 2024
In this summer project, I formulated a general procedure for constructing quantum error-correcting codes through the irreducible representations of SU(d) for any odd integer d≥3. This builds upon the work done by Gross for SU(2) and Herbert et al. for SU(3). The key to our generalisation was the exploitation of two forms of symmetry. Details can be found in our preprint.
